About Me

My name is Heondo Kim, I am an aspiring web developer located in Irvine, CA.
Growing up I always loved math for a variety of reasons: the rules of math were clearly defined, the solution is always the same, and how you approach the problem is open. It made a lot of sense to me, I knew how to get A's on a math test but I could never quite figure out the same for an essay.

I graduated from UCSB with a Financial Math and Stats degree and came out with a new interest, programming. I think it's very straightforward why I liked programming: the rules are clearly defined, the solution is always usually the same, and you can approach the problem however you like. With the added bonus of being automated.
